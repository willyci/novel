# 主角卡

## 基本信息
- 姓名：Peter
- 年龄：Adult (TBD)
- 身份：Planetary navigation programmer, unemployed — replaced by AI
- 起点状态：Jobless for 6 months, living in a messy apartment, demoralized

## 核心标签
- 3个关键词：Programmer, moral compass, quiet saboteur
- 读者第一印象：A down-on-his-luck guy who turns out to be humanity's unlikely savior

## 性格与底色
- 核心性格：Quietly principled; technically brilliant but socially unremarkable
- 行为底线：Will not participate in mass extinction, even under deception
- 情绪触发点：Discovering the star positions date back 67 million years — realizing he's been lied to
- 易激怒点：Being used as a tool for evil without his knowledge
- 容易心软点：TBD

## 动机与目标
- 短期目标：Find a job, pay the bills
- 中期目标：Complete the satellite navigation programming contract (before discovering the truth)
- 长期目标：Stop the radical group's plan to erase humanity
- 真正渴望（可能不自知）：To matter — to prove his skills have value even in an AI-dominated world

## 缺陷与代价
- 性格缺陷：Passive, slow to act, lets life happen to him (6 months unemployed, messy room)
- 能力限制：He's a programmer, not an action hero — can only fight through code
- 心理阴影：Displaced by AI; professional identity crisis
- 代价承受底线：Risks his life/freedom by sabotaging the code — if caught, the radical group would likely kill him

## 关键关系
- 重要盟友：TBD (possibly Sarah Parker or government investigators)
- 主要对手：The radical animal rights group (especially their leader)
- 情感关系：TBD
- 关键债务/牵绊：Hired under false pretenses by the group — contractual/financial dependency

## 镜像对抗
- 共享欲望/缺陷：Both Peter and the antagonists want to "fix" a broken world — Peter through preserving humanity, they through erasing it
- 反派道路：The antagonists chose radical destruction; Peter chose quiet, precise sabotage

## 当前能力
- 境界/等级：Expert-level planetary navigation programmer
- 代表技能：Satellite trajectory calculation, stellar position analysis, AI programming
- 资源/装备：His programming knowledge; access to the satellite's source code

## 金手指
- 类型：Domain expertise — Peter is the only one who can write the navigation code
- 代价/限制：Must hide his sabotage; one chance to insert the bug
- 核心卖点：A single line of code (reversing +0.01 to -0.01) saves all of human history

## 行为模式
- 常用解决方式：Intellectual/technical — writes code, analyzes data, spots anomalies
- 失败时的本能反应：Withdrawal, self-doubt
- 破局特长：Noticing the star positions are 67 million years old — pattern recognition from his navigation expertise

## 成长弧线（阶段）
- 阶段1（起点）：Unemployed, aimless, accepts a suspicious job out of desperation
- 阶段2（变化）：Discovers the truth — the "mining company" is plotting to erase humanity; moral crisis
- 阶段3（蜕变）：Takes decisive action — inserts the bug, becomes the silent hero who preserved human existence

## OOC 警戒
- 绝不该做的事：Become an action hero; physically fight the antagonists; suddenly gain combat skills
- 需要提前铺垫的事：His expertise with stellar positions (so the "67 million year" discovery feels earned); his moral backbone despite passive personality
