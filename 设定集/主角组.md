# 主角组设定

## 主角列表
| 名称 | 定位 | 主线贡献 | 关键缺陷 | 关键能力 |
|------|------|----------|----------|----------|
| Peter | 主线protagonist | Sabotages the navigation code, saves humanity | Passive, displaced by AI, slow to act | Planetary navigation programming, stellar cartography |
| Sarah Parker | POV character (prologue/framing) | Introduces the world and thematic stakes | Haunted by loss (husband/son), distracted | Teacher, connects readers to the human cost |

| 名称 | 角色 | 功能 |
|------|------|------|

## 共同目标
- 团队共同目标：N/A — this is not a team story; Peter acts alone
- 阶段性目标：Peter's solo arc from employment → discovery → sabotage

## 角色分工
- 决策者：Peter (ultimately)
- 执行者：Peter (code insertion)
- 信息中枢：The antagonist group (controls information asymmetry)
- 情感支点：Sarah Parker (thematic mirror — loss, nostalgia, what humanity means)

## 视角与节奏
- POV 分配比例：Peter ~50%, Sarah ~15%, Steve/Bob/Joe ~20%, antagonists ~15%
- 轮换规则：Each chapter follows a different character to build the heist puzzle from multiple angles
- 防止抢戏的约束：Peter's chapters carry the main emotional weight; other POVs serve the plot machinery

## 内部冲突
- 价值观冲突：Antagonists' environmentalism vs. Peter's humanism
- 资源冲突：N/A
- 信任裂缝：Peter doesn't trust his employers; they don't suspect his sabotage

## 团队成长弧线
- 组建期：Various characters unknowingly contribute to the heist (Steve, Joe, Dr. Freeman)
- 磨合期：Peter is brought in as the final piece
- 裂变期/重组期：Peter's betrayal shatters the plan from within
