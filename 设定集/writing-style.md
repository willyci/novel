# Writing Style Guide: Ben Bova's Approach (本·波瓦写作风格指南)

## 1. Tone and Core Philosophy (基调与核心理念)
- **Optimistic Technophilia (乐观的技术狂热):** Technology is the ultimate tool for human liberation and progress, not a villain. Approach scientific advancement with a sense of wonder, hope, and determination.
- **Rigorous Scientific Realism (严谨的科学写实):** Root all technology and scenarios in plausible, near-future science. Avoid "space magic." Rely on actual physics, engineering, and known scientific principles.
- **Human-Driven Progress (人类驱动的进步):** The universe isn't actively trying to kill humanity; human short-sightedness is. Focus on the ambition, ingenuity, and sheer willpower of the characters pushing boundaries.

## 2. Narrative Focus & Conflict (叙事焦点与冲突)
- **Melodramatic & High-Stakes Human Conflict (高风险与人际冲突):** Frame the story around intense human drama—corporate espionage, deep political rivalries, and romantic tension. The characters' personal stakes are inextricably linked to the success or failure of the project.
- **Political and Corporate Friction (政治与企业摩擦):** The primary antagonists are rarely aliens or cosmic anomalies. The real conflict comes from bureaucratic red tape, corporate greed, political maneuvering, sabotage, and funding struggles that threaten progress.
- **Professional Competence (专业主义):** Characters should be highly competent professionals—scientists, engineers, astronauts, executives—who solve problems using logic, diplomacy, and technical know-how.
- **Near-Future Focus (近未来视角):** Ground the setting in the next logical steps for humanity (e.g., lunar colonization, asteroid mining, solving Earth's resource crises) rather than far-future space fantasy.

## 3. Pacing and Structure (节奏与结构)
- **The Countdown Timer (倒计时机制):** Set the narrative against a ticking clock. Whether it's an impending budget vote, a dwindling oxygen supply, or a corporate hostile takeover deadline, there must be a constant, looming countdown to drive relentless urgency.
- **Procedural Pacing (流程驱动的节奏):** The plot often moves forward through the achievement of technical milestones, tense boardroom negotiations, and the execution of complex engineering tasks.
- **Tension in the Details (细节中的张力):** Suspense should arise from plausible technical failures (a blown oxygen valve, a miscalculated trajectory, a sudden budget cut) or political backstabbing.

## 4. Dialogue Rules (对话规则)
- **Professional & Purposeful (专业且目的明确):** Dialogue should reflect the characters' professional backgrounds. Use accurate jargon naturally, focusing the conversation on the stakes (e.g., arguing over the cost of life support versus its efficiency).
- **Ideological Clashes (理念冲突):** Conversations often double as ideological battles between visionaries (scientists/explorers) and pragmatists/obstructionists (bureaucrats/politicians/rival CEOs).

## 5. Description Guidelines (描写指南)
- **Utilitarian & Clear (实用与清晰):** Describe machinery and environments with the precision of a technical editor. Focus on function, scale, and the stark contrast between the harshness of space and the fragility of human habitats.
- **The Awe of Achievement (成就的敬畏感):** When describing a new technology or space vista, emphasize the human effort and engineering mastery it took to achieve it. The beauty lies in the mechanics.

## 6. Formatting & World-Building (排版与世界观构建)
- **In-Universe Documents:** Consider using interspersed documents (corporate memos, political briefs, news headlines, budget reports) at the beginning of chapters to quickly establish the political climate and world-building.
- **Time/Location Stamps:** Ground the story in a very specific, plausible timeline with clear location tags (e.g., **[Location: UN Assembly Space Committee | Date: October 14]**).

## 7. Vocabulary / Banned Concepts (词汇/禁用概念)
- **Key Themes/Words:** Logistics, infrastructure, thermodynamics, orbital mechanics, senate hearings, patents, venture capital, payload capacity.
- **Banned Concepts:** Unexplained faster-than-light (FTL) travel, humanoid aliens, mystical energy fields, "evil" AI (unless born from logical corporate programming flaws), science fantasy tropes.
