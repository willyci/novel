# 世界观设定

## 世界一句话
In 2543, humanity has fled a ruined Earth to colonize Mars, where advanced technology enables time travel—but moral extremism threatens to erase human existence entirely.

## 题材融合点（复合题材可填）
- 主题材：Hard Sci-Fi (time travel, nanotechnology)
- 副题材：Moral thriller / techno-heist
- 融合机制：Hard science (micro black holes, nanomachines, orbital mechanics) provides the mechanism; moral philosophy provides the stakes.

## 世界结构
- 大陆/位面数量：Solar system — Earth (declining), Mars (civilization hub), Kuiper Belt (mining frontier)
- 核心区域：Mars — primary human settlement, research labs, corporate HQs
- 边缘区域：Kuiper Belt — asteroid mining operations, lawless frontier; Earth — degraded but still has museums/historical sites

## 势力格局
- 核心势力：
  - Mars Government — regulates technology, tracks particle accelerators, enforces animal contraband laws
  - SpaceNano Corporation — dominant mining company, CEO Nova Synapse, deploys nanomachine tech to asteroids
  - National Energy Laboratory — government-backed, develops "Sun 2.0" clean energy, director Michael Jones
- 次级势力：
  - Radical animal rights group (unnamed) — underground extremists, the antagonist faction
  - Black market networks — Joe and others, trade in contraband (animals, stolen tech)
- 敌对/中立关系：Government vs. black market; animal rights group operates in secret against all of society
- 宗门/组织层级：Corporate hierarchy (CEO/directors/engineers/workers); Government bureaucracy; Underground cell structure for the radical group

## 社会结构
- 社会阶层：Corporate elite (CEOs, directors) > Scientists/Engineers > Workers (Bob, Steve) > Displaced workers (Peter, replaced by AI)
- 资源分配规则：Corporate-driven economy; mining resources shipped from Kuiper Belt to Mars; AI replacing human labor
- 信仰/意识形态：Mainstream — human progress and survival; Radical fringe — animal life is sacred, humanity is a plague

## 历史年表（关键节点）
- ~2200s: Earth's environmental collapse accelerates, Mars colonization begins
- ~2400s: Mars becomes primary civilization hub; genetic modifications for Mars-born humans
- ~2500s: Kuiper Belt mining boom; SpaceNano pioneers nanomachine mining
- 2543: Present day — AI displaces human workers; "Sun 2.0" energy project active; radical group begins their heist plan
- 67 million years ago: Target era — the Chicxulub asteroid that killed the dinosaurs

## 核心规则
- 资源稀缺性：Energy is the bottleneck — micro black holes require enormous instantaneous energy release ("Sun 2.0")
- 政治/宗门规则：Government tracks all particle accelerators; animal ownership is illegal on Mars (100 sols imprisonment)
- 社会常识/禁忌：AI has replaced dangerous/remote human jobs; DNA traces must be destroyed after illegal transactions
- 硬约束（不可违背）：
  - Time travel requires: particle accelerator + exotic matter energy source + nanomachine payload
  - Nanomachines require complex activation process — dormant without it
  - Once sent through the black hole, no recall or communication is possible
  - The 0.01 degree change is the minimum viable deflection — no precision beyond that

## 世界运转机制
- 能量/资源循环：Asteroids mined by nanomachines → ingots shipped to Moon Station 19 → distributed to Mars
- 技术/法术基础：Nanotechnology (self-replicating machines), AI navigation, micro black hole generation, genetic modification, neural implants
- 公平性与代价规则：Technology is powerful but requires massive infrastructure; stolen tech is useless without activation codes/energy sources

## 货币与经济
- 货币体系：Credits (digital currency)
- 兑换规则：Standard solar economy
- 主要流通形态：Electronic credits
- 交易场景：Corporate contracts, black market deals (Joe's transactions), worker wages

## 地理版图
- 重要地点清单：
  - Earth's Natural History Museum (field trip location, Ch1)
  - Mars warehouse/logistics hub (Ch2, nanomachine theft)
  - Mars Energy Research Laboratory (Ch4, "Sun 2.0" location)
  - Old particle accelerator facility (Ch3, near a power plant)
  - Secret Kuiper Belt asteroid lab (antagonist base)
  - Moon Station 19 (mining ingot receiving station)
- 关键资源点：Kuiper Belt asteroids (mining); Mars labs (technology)

## 日常生活
- 货币/交易方式：Credits (digital); black market uses physical exchanges
- 交通/通信：Spaceships between planets; neural implant communication; TV news broadcasts
- 教育/职业体系：Schools with field trips (Sarah's class); programmers, warehouse workers, scientists; AI rapidly displacing human jobs (planetary navigators)
