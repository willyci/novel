# 金手指设计

## 设定定位
- 题材适配点：Hard sci-fi — the "golden finger" is knowledge, not a supernatural power
- 主要作用：Resolves the central conflict through a single act of code sabotage
- 读者可见度：暗牌 (hidden from other characters; revealed to the reader at the critical moment)

## 类型
Domain Expertise — Peter's specialized knowledge of planetary navigation and stellar cartography

## 核心功能
- 功能描述：Peter can calculate and program satellite trajectories using stellar positions. This unique skill lets him: (1) detect the 67-million-year-old star chart anomaly, (2) insert a conditional bug that reverses the trajectory
- 可视化表现：The code itself — a conditional statement: "if timestamp is in the past, multiply trajectory adjustment by -1"
- 触发条件：Being given access to the satellite navigation source code

## 获得方式
- 触发事件：Years of training and career as a planetary navigator, now unemployed due to AI replacement
- 代价/限制：His expertise is narrow — useless outside navigation/orbital mechanics
- 不可逆代价：If caught, the radical group will kill him; he has no escape plan

## 使用规则
- 冷却/次数限制：One shot — he gets one chance to insert the bug before the code is deployed
- 禁止事项：Cannot openly fight or flee; must maintain the facade of loyal employee
- 失败惩罚：If the bug is detected, humanity's existence is at stake
- 反制方式（敌人如何克制）：Code review by another expert could catch the bug — but Peter is the only domain expert they have

## 升级路线
- 初始形态：Passive knowledge (unemployed programmer)
- 中期提升：Applied knowledge (discovers the truth through star position analysis)
- 最终形态：Decisive action (inserts the bug that saves humanity)

## 爽点嵌入
- 获得爽：The moment Peter realizes the star positions are 67 million years old — "they're not mining asteroids, they're changing history"
- 成长爽：Peter choosing to act despite fear — from passive observer to silent hero
- 使用爽：The reveal that the 0.01 became -0.01, and the asteroid still hits Earth, preserving human history

## 反馈节奏
- 关键反馈节点：(1) Peter hired (setup), (2) Star chart discovery (revelation), (3) Bug insertion (climax), (4) Mission "fails" (payoff)
- 代价兑现节点：Peter's danger after the group realizes their plan failed
- 反转节点：The antagonists think they succeeded — then realize the trajectory went the wrong way

## 与创意约束对齐
- 反套路规则如何体现：The hero doesn't fight, doesn't have powers — he writes one line of code
- 硬约束如何绑定金手指：Peter's expertise is the ONLY thing that could stop the plan — no deus ex machina
- 主角缺陷如何被放大/修复：His passivity nearly lets him become complicit; his moral backbone forces him to act
