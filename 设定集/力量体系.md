# 力量体系设定

## 体系公理
- 核心信条/定律：Technology is the sole source of power; no magic, no supernatural. All capabilities derive from engineering and physics.
- 代价规则：Every technology has a resource cost — energy, time, infrastructure, or moral compromise.
- 公平性原则：Anyone with knowledge and resources can wield technology; the radical group's advantage comes from stolen tech, not innate ability.

## 体系类型
- 体系类型：Hard sci-fi technology tiers
- 典型境界链：N/A (no cultivation; technology levels instead)
- 小境界划分：N/A

## 能力来源
- 能量/资源来源：Solar energy, asteroid minerals, "Sun 2.0" experimental energy
- 训练/修炼方法：Scientific education, programming skill, engineering expertise
- 社会控制机制：Corporations control tech deployment; government tracks dangerous equipment (particle accelerators)

## 等级体系 (Technology Tiers)
- Tier 1 — Consumer tech: Neural implants, genetic modifications, basic AI
- Tier 2 — Industrial tech: Self-replicating nanomachines, asteroid mining systems, AI planetary navigation
- Tier 3 — Experimental tech: "Sun 2.0" energy source, particle accelerator (micro black hole generation)
- Tier 4 — Theoretical/forbidden: Time travel via micro black hole + nanomachine payload

## 晋级条件
- 资源要求：Each tier requires exponentially more energy and infrastructure
- 突破方式：Scientific breakthroughs + resource acquisition
- 失败代价：Lab explosions (National Energy Lab incident), job loss (Peter), imprisonment (100 sols for contraband)
- 越级代价：Stealing tech = criminal prosecution; using experimental tech without proper setup = catastrophic failure

## 资源系统
- 核心资源类型：
  - Energy (especially instantaneous high-energy release for black holes)
  - Nanomachines (self-replicating, but require activation)
  - Exotic matter ("Sun 2.0")
  - Particle accelerators (create micro black holes for ~7 milliseconds)
  - Programming expertise (Peter's satellite navigation code)
- 资源获取方式：Corporate supply chains, theft, black market
- 稀缺性规则："Sun 2.0" is one-of-a-kind experimental; particle accelerators are government-tracked
- 替代路径/灰色交易：Steve/Joe black market chain; buying decommissioned equipment under false pretenses (Dr. Freeman)

## Key Technologies

### Self-Replicating Nanomachines
- Designed for asteroid mining
- Dormant without complex activation process ("just like dust")
- One survivor can bootstrap: mine → replicate → build larger machines → survey → refine → ship
- Shot from cannons to asteroid surfaces in glass tubes
- Over centuries: can self-replicate into a functional satellite with scanning + ion propulsion

### "Sun 2.0"
- Experimental clean energy source from National Energy Laboratory
- Can release all energy in an instant — required for micro black hole generation
- Stolen by radical group (lab explosion as cover)

### Particle Accelerator
- Creates micro black holes lasting ~7 milliseconds
- Requires enormous power (originally located next to a power plant)
- Government tracks all units; old model acquired under false pretenses

### Micro Black Hole (Time Travel Mechanism)
- Generated by particle accelerator + "Sun 2.0" energy
- Nanomachine passes through → emerges 67 million years in the past
- One-way trip; no communication possible after transit

## 禁忌与限制
- 禁术/禁地：Time travel itself is the ultimate forbidden technology
- 高阶限制：Micro black holes last only 7 milliseconds — extremely narrow window
- 硬限制（不可绕过）：
  - Cannot communicate with the past once nanomachine is sent
  - Nanomachine must operate autonomously for ~1 million years
  - Satellite cannot verify trajectory while in asteroid's shadow
  - 0.01 degree precision is the limit of ion propulsion deflection

## 体系漏洞与利用
- 漏洞：The satellite navigation AI trusts its programming blindly — if the code says -0.01, it executes -0.01
- 主角如何利用：Peter inserts a conditional bug — if timestamp is in the past, reverse the trajectory sign
- 反派如何反制：They don't catch the bug because Peter is the domain expert they hired
