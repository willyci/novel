# 反派设计

## 基本信息
- 名称：Radical Animal Rights Group (leader TBD — possibly the mysterious man in shadows)
- 身份/势力：Underground extremist cell operating from Kuiper Belt
- 出场时机：Their agents appear from Ch1 (mysterious stranger at museum) onward

## 反派分层（必须）
| 层级 | 名称 | 阶段 | 目标 | 与主角关系 |
|------|------|------|------|------------|
| 小反派 | Joe (middleman) | Early (Ch2) | Acquire nanomachines via black market | Indirect — procures stolen tech |
| 小反派 | "Dr. Freeman" (false identity) | Early (Ch3) | Acquire particle accelerator under false pretenses | Indirect — secures equipment |
| 中反派 | The mysterious man in shadows | Mid | Orchestrates the heist — steals nanomachines, Sun 2.0, recruits Peter | Direct employer of Peter |
| 大反派 | Group leader (TBD) | Late | Execute the time travel plan to erase humanity | Peter's ultimate adversary |

## 核心驱动
- 核心欲望：Undo humanity's destruction of Earth; save animal life (specifically dinosaurs as symbol)
- 核心恐惧：Humanity will continue destroying all life if allowed to exist
- 行动原则：The ends justify the means — willing to erase billions of humans to "save" animals

## 镜像对抗
- 与主角共享欲望/缺陷：Both believe the current world is broken and want to fix it
- 反派道路：They choose extinction (erase humanity); Peter chooses preservation (protect humanity through sabotage)
- 价值观冲突点：Is human existence worth preserving given its destruction of the environment? The antagonists say no; Peter says yes

## 能力与资源
- 实力层级：Highly organized; access to black market networks, false identities, scientific knowledge
- 关键能力/手段：
  - Social engineering (manipulating Steve, hiring Peter under false pretenses)
  - Technical expertise (activating dormant nanomachines, operating particle accelerator)
  - Operational security (DNA trace burning, compartmentalized knowledge)
- 组织/资源支持：Secret lab on Kuiper Belt asteroid; enough funding for black market purchases and hiring specialists

## 规则与代价
- 被限制的规则：Need specific rare tech (Sun 2.0, accelerator, nanomachines) — can't create from scratch
- 代价机制：The heist itself is high-risk; lab explosion (National Energy Lab) caused a casualty (Mike the security guard)
- 反制点（主角可利用）：They MUST hire an outside expert (Peter) for navigation code — creating their vulnerability

## 关键剧情节点
- 第一次正面交锋：Peter realizes the star positions are 67 million years old
- 中期压制点：Peter is trapped — working for them with no easy way out
- 失败/反转节点：The nanomachine is sent through the black hole, but Peter's bug reverses the trajectory
- 终局命运：Plan fails; they realize immediately and begin plotting a new, more sinister scheme

## 复杂度
- 是否可洗白/转化：Philosophically sympathetic (they're not wrong about environmental destruction) but methods are inexcusable
- 是否存在更高层反派：Possible — "even more sinister plan" teased at the end

## 反派进阶路径
- 反派升级/换挡节奏：Small agents (Joe, fake Dr. Freeman) → mysterious orchestrator → full group reveal at Kuiper Belt lab
- 权力阶梯/势力扩张方式：Accumulate stolen technology piece by piece; expand from Mars black market to Kuiper Belt base
