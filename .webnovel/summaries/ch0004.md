## Plot Summary
Building 7 at the National Energy Lab explodes at 02:17 on Sol 15, killing night guard Mike Reeves. Captain Elena Voss leads the investigation; Director Michael Jones is devastated. The official conclusion: containment failure in the Sun 2.0 exotic matter housing. A flashback reveals the truth -- Dr. Lena Park sabotaged sensor thresholds fourteen hours prior, and a hijacked maintenance drone extracted the Sun 2.0 prototype twenty-seven minutes before the blast. The explosion was not the accident but the cover. Three days later, all three stolen technologies (nanomachines, Kepler-7 accelerator, Sun 2.0) converge on a single cargo shuttle piloted by Dex, bound for the Kuiper Belt. The chapter closes with a job posting for a navigation programmer -- the final piece of the conspiracy.
