## Plot Summary
Peter Kamau, an expert satellite navigation programmer unemployed for six months after Helix Astronautics replaced him with AI, lies in his deteriorating apartment in Olympus City. TV news covers SpaceNano's nanomachine expansion (CEO Nova Synapse) and the National Energy Lab explosion aftermath (Director Michael Jones, victim Mike Reeves). Peter receives a targeted recruitment listing from Astra Talent Solutions via the Martian Employment Exchange: Satellite Navigation Programmer, 85K credits, confidential client. Despite red flags (no company name, no address, suspiciously fast response), desperation and the listing's precise match to his skill set compel him to accept the interview. He begins cleaning his apartment -- the first sign of hope in months.
