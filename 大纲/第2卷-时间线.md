## 时间跳跃规则

### Allowed time progression
- Same day continuous: no special transition needed
- Overnight (6-12 hours): 1-2 sentence transition
- Multi-day skip (1-7 days): transition paragraph showing passage of time (work montage, routine establishing)
- Large skip (>7 days): must be the inter-chapter gap with clear time marker

### Forbidden time operations
- ❌ Time reversal (unless explicit flashback)
- ❌ Events contradicting established timeline
- ❌ Unexplained large jumps within a chapter

## Notes
- The multi-day gaps between chapters are intentional: they represent Peter settling into routine, doing his work, building relationships. Each chapter is a "significant day" — a day when something changes.
- Sol 40-68 covers ~4 weeks of base life, giving Peter enough time to: arrive and orient (Ch6), begin working (Ch7), encounter the cover story (Ch8), accept and belong (Ch9), discover the truth (Ch10).
- The final chapter (Ch10) spans 48 hours to allow the discovery process and its emotional aftermath.
