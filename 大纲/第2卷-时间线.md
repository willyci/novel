## Time Progression Rules

### Allowed
- Multi-day work montages: Peter's coding work compresses naturally (Ch7 covers days of routine)
- The 13-day gap between Ch9 and Ch10: Peter must continue working normally while planning his sabotage — this period of hidden tension is implied, not shown
- Sol numbering continues from Volume 1 (Sol 22 was Ch5)

### Forbidden
- No time reversal (all events are forward-progressing)
- No unexplained compression of the code deadline countdown
