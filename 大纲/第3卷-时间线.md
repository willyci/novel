## 时间跳跃规则

### Allowed
- Sol 70-80: 10-day launch countdown with multi-day gaps (routine prep work between key events)
- Ch13 → Ch14: Massive time jump to 67 million years ago (clearly marked POV/time shift)
- Ch14 → Ch15: Return to present timeline (Sol 83); 3 days of post-launch monitoring compressed
- Ch14's million-year span: Compressed poetic narration, not real-time

### Special: Chapter 14 Time Structure
Ch14 breaks the linear timeline to show the nanomachine's journey in the deep past. This is the ONLY non-linear chapter in the entire novel. It should be clearly framed as a departure — possibly opening with a time stamp ("67,000,000 years before the present") and returning to the present at the chapter's end or the start of Ch15.

### Forbidden
- ❌ Time reversal within the present-day timeline
- ❌ Unexplained gaps in the countdown (D-10 must reach D-0 monotonically)
