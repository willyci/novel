## Time Progression Rules

### Allowed
- Multi-day gaps between chapters: Each chapter follows a different heist thread, so time gaps of days are natural
- Cross-cutting between locations: Mars surface, warehouses, labs, Peter's apartment
- The Prologue is a deep-time flash (67M years ago) — justified as cosmic scene-setting

### Forbidden
- No time reversal (except the Prologue's deep-time opening)
- No unexplained compression (events within a single chapter should feel real-time)
