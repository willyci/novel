# 第 2 卷：The Truth - 节拍表

> 章节范围: 第 6 - 10 章
> 核心冲突: Peter works on a project he believes is revolutionary mining tech, but the truth is far worse — and discovering it traps him between complicity and conscience
> 卷末高潮: Peter discovers the star positions are 67 million years old; the cover story crumbles; he realizes the group plans to erase humanity

## 1) 开卷承诺（Promise）
- 本卷读者承诺：
  1. Meet the Kuiper Belt base crew and see the conspiracy's inner workings (cognitive satisfaction)
  2. Watch Peter be seduced by a brilliant cover story, knowing the reader already knows it's a lie (dramatic irony escalation)
  3. The moment Peter sees through the lie — the 67-million-year revelation (cognitive payoff, intensity 7+)
- 主要兑现类型：信息（cover story mechanics + truth revelation）、情绪（Peter's belonging → betrayal）

## 2) 催化事件（Catalyst）
- 事件：Peter arrives at the Kuiper Belt asteroid base and meets the crew — a small, close-knit team of specialists running what appears to be a legitimate (if secretive) deep-space mining R&D operation
- 不可逆变化：Peter is now physically isolated from Mars, dependent on the group for survival; there is no easy way back
- 主角当下目标：Do the job well, earn the pay, prove his skills still matter

## 3) 升级危机链（Fichtean 危机递增，至少 3 次）
| 节点 | 危机/冲突 | 代价/风险升级 | 结果/变化 |
|------|-----------|---------------|-----------|
| 1 | Peter notices the base has no corporate oversight, no safety inspectors, no communication with any known mining company | Professional unease — this doesn't match any legitimate operation he's seen | Peter raises concerns; the Handler deflects smoothly |
| 2 | Peter's nav code requirements include "stellar drift correction over millions of years" — far beyond any normal mining trajectory | Intellectual dissonance — the physics is real but the scale is unprecedented | Peter questions the bizarre requirement; the Professor delivers the cover story |
| 3 | Peter accepts the cover story but notices the star chart data is oddly specific — not generic millions-of-years-ago positions but a very particular epoch | The cover story's seams begin to show; Peter's expertise is the exact tool that can detect the lie | Peter begins running his own calculations in private |
| 4 | Peter confirms: the stellar positions correspond to 67 million years ago — the Cretaceous extinction era. The trajectory target isn't an interceptor orbit; it aligns with the Chicxulub asteroid's approach path | The truth: they're not mining asteroids. They're redirecting the extinction asteroid. If the nanomachine deflects it by 0.01 degrees, it misses Earth, dinosaurs survive, humanity never evolves | Peter realizes he's been coding the extinction of the human race |

## 4) 中段反转（必填）
- 假胜利：Peter accepts the cover story and experiences genuine belonging — he's valued, his skills matter, the Professor treats him like a colleague. For the first time since Helix fired him, Peter feels like he has a purpose. He even starts to believe in the "mission."
- 反转带来的新认知：The belonging was built on a lie. The very expertise that made Peter feel valued is what lets him detect the deception. His competence becomes his curse — he cannot unsee what the star chart tells him.

## 5) 卷末最低谷（All Is Lost）
- 最低谷事件：Peter confirms the truth but is trapped on an asteroid base weeks from Mars, surrounded by people who would kill him if they knew he'd discovered their plan. He has no allies, no communication, no escape.
- 代价：Peter's sense of belonging is destroyed; his purpose is inverted — the work he was proud of is coding genocide
- 主角选择：Peter must decide whether to continue pretending, confront the group, or find a way to sabotage from within — but this decision is NOT made in Vol 2. The volume ends with him paralyzed by the knowledge.

## 6) 卷末大兑现 + 新钩子（Payoff + Next Promise）
- 本卷兑现：
  1. The cover story reveal — a brilliantly constructed lie that the reader watches Peter accept (dramatic irony payoff)
  2. The 67-million-year star chart discovery — the volume's cognitive climax; Peter's expertise cracks the lie (intensity 7)
  3. The full picture assembled — reader now understands the complete plan: nanomachines + time travel + asteroid deflection = human extinction
- 新钩子：Peter knows the truth but hasn't acted. Will he sabotage the code? Can he do it without being caught? Does he even have the moral certainty to condemn an entire species (the dinosaurs) to extinction in order to preserve humanity?
- 章末未闭合问题：Peter sits alone with the star chart data, knowing what it means, unable to tell anyone, unable to leave, unable to decide what to do.
